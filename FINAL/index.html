Our Story

Two Decades of Building Careers

SSS College of Arts, Science and Management, Arcot was established in 2005 and is affiliated to Thiruvalluvar University, Vellore. Spread over 5.11 acres in Arcot, Ranipet district, the college is a private, co-educational institution.

With 13 UG & PG programmes across Arts, Science, Commerce and Management, SSS College has produced thousands of graduates now working across India's top companies and government services.

The college is affiliated to Thiruvalluvar University — a NAAC accredited, UGC-approved university and A.I.U member, located at Serkkadu, Vellore – 632 115, Tamil Nadu.

Admissions 2026–27

A simple, student-friendly admission process for all UG & PG programmes at SSS College, Arcot.

How to Apply

Admission Process

1

Choose Programme

Browse our 12 UG & PG programmes and shortlist the course matching your interests and career goals.

→
2

Check Eligibility

Confirm you meet the eligibility criteria for your chosen UG or PG programme (see below).

→
3

Fill Application

Fill the online enquiry form, or collect a physical form from the admissions office.

→
4

Submit Documents

Submit with T.C., mark sheets, 2 community certificate copies, and 2 passport-size photos.

→
5

Pay Fee & Confirm

Pay admission fee + University Special Fee (₹1,500 UG / ₹2,000 PG) and get your admission.

Fill Online Enquiry Form →

🎓 UG Eligibility

A pass in the +2 examination conducted by the Government of Tamil Nadu, or an equivalent examination from other State Boards.

🎓 PG Eligibility

A pass in any UG degree with Mathematics, Statistics, or Computer Science as one of the subjects studied (for M.Sc CS and M.Com).

💰 Fee Concessions

  • +2 marks 600: 10% off per semester
  • SC/ST, Farmer, Minority scholarships available
  • Pudumai Pen & Tamil Pudhalvan schemes

📄 Documents Required

  • Original Transfer Certificate (T.C.)
  • Original mark sheets (+2 or UG)
  • 2 copies of community certificate
  • 2 recent passport-size photographs

Campus Life

News & Events

-

Annual Sports Day

Students competed across Volleyball, Football, Hockey and Athletics. The event culminated with championship awards and an inspiring pyramid formation display by the physical education students.

-

Campus Placement Drive

Our Placement Cell organised campus drives with TVS. Several students received appointment letters before completing their final semester.

-

Annual Day & College Day

The Annual Day and College Day celebrations were marked by vibrant cultural performances — music, dance, drama and a grand prize-giving ceremony.

-

Convocation & University Rank

SSS College students were honoured at Thiruvalluvar University's Convocation ceremony. We proudly announce a University 1st Rank holder!

-

Onam Celebration

SSS College celebrated Onam with spectacular floral pookalam and cultural programmes. Students participated in traditional games, folk dances and a festive feast.

-

Inter-College Karate Championship

SSS College students participated in inter-college Karate championships, bringing home medals. Our physical education department continues to produce state-level athletes.

-

National Youth Day

National Youth Day was observed with great enthusiasm — featuring speeches, essay competitions and talks on Swami Vivekananda's life and teachings.

-

Pongal Celebration

Pongal was celebrated with traditional rituals, kolam art, sugarcane and the boiling of the sacred Pongal pot. The festival united the entire campus community.

-

NSS Special Camp

Our NSS unit ran a special camp focused on community and social-service initiatives around Arcot — cleanliness drives, awareness programmes and village outreach.

-

Graduation Day

Graduating students were honoured at our Graduation Day. Parents, faculty and trustees joined to celebrate the achievements of the outgoing batch.

-

Deepavali Celebration

Deepavali was celebrated on campus with traditional sweets, lamp lighting ceremonies and cultural entertainment. The campus was lit with joy and colours.
